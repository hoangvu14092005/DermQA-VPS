# **Telangiectasia**

Author: A/Prof Amanda Oakley, Dermatologist, Hamilton, New Zealand, 2014.

---

## What is telangiectasia?

Telangiectasia is a condition in which there are visible small linear red blood vessels (broken capillaries ). These are also called telangiectases. Visible small blood vessels that are blue in colour (spider veins) are called venulectasia because venules are involved.

## What is the differential diagnosis of telangiectasia?

Telangiectases need to be distinguished from other [vascular conditions](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/vascular-skin-problems) , including blood vessel tumours such as [infantile haemangioma](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/infantile-haemangioma-definition-and-pathogenesis) and [angiomas](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/cherry-angioma) that arise in adults; and [capillary](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/capillary-vascular-malformation) or [venous vascular malformations](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/venous-malformation) .
Large red blood vessels are arteries and large blue blood vessels are veins. Arteries may be enlarged due to aneurysm formation. Veins enlarged due to the destruction of their valvular system are known as [varicose veins](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/varicose-veins) .

## Classification of telangiectasia


### Inherited conditions
- [Hereditary haemorrhagic telangiectasia](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/hereditary-haemorrhagic-telangiectasia)
- [Benign hereditary telangiectasia](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/hereditary-benign-telangiectasia)
- Unilateral naevoid telangiectasia
- [Angioma serpiginosum](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/angioma-serpiginosum)
- [Rothmund Thompson syndrome](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/rothmund-thomson-syndrome)
- [Ataxia-telangiectasia](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/ataxia-telangiectasia)
- [Bloom syndrome](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/bloom-syndrome) ( congenital telangiectatic erythema )
- Cockayne syndrome
- [Cutis marmorata telangiectatica congenita](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/cutis-marmorata)
- [Focal dermal hypoplasia (Goltz syndrome)](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/focal-dermal-hypoplasia)
- Kindler syndrome
Note that telangiectasia may be noted as a normal feature of facial skin in some families.

- [Sun damaged and aged skin](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/ageing-skin) especially in those who [smoke](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/smoking-and-its-effects-on-the-skin)
- [Spider telangiectasis](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/spider-telangiectasis) (also called spider naevus )
- Progressive annular telangiectasia (a rare condition with no associations)
- [Poikiloderma of Civatte](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/poikiloderma-of-civatte) (sun damage affecting the sides of the neck)
- [Pregnancy](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/skin-changes-in-pregnancy)
- [Liver disease](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/skin-manifestations-of-gastrointestinal-disease) particularly when associated with [alcohol](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/cutaneous-adverse-effects-of-alcohol) or [viral infection](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/viral-hepatitis)
- [Cushing syndrome](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/cushing-syndrome)
- [Xơ cứng hệ thống](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/systemic-sclerosis) đặc biệt là hội chứng CRST (tạo thành các mảng giãn mạch)
- [Bệnh lupus ban đỏ da](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/cutaneous-lupus-erythematosus)
- [Bệnh mô liên kết hỗn hợp](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/mixed-connective-tissue-disease)
- [Viêm da cơ](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/adult-onset-dermatomyositis)
- [Bệnh tăng sinh tế bào mast](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/mastocytosis) , cụ thể là biến thể, telangiectasia macularis eruptiva perstans
- [Hội chứng carcinoid](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/carcinoid-syndrome)
- [Bệnh hoại tử mỡ](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/necrobiosis-lipoidica)
- [Rối loạn cảm giác ở bộ phận sinh dục nam](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/male-genital-dysaesthesia)
- Bệnh lý mạch máu collagen ở da - mô học cho thấy collagen hyalin hóa bao quanh các mạch máu giãn mạch

## Phương pháp điều trị giãn mao mạch là gì?

Giãn mao mạch thường vô hại. Có thể cần điều trị nếu xuất hiện tình trạng chảy máu hoặc trông không đẹp mắt. Các phương pháp [điều trị giãn mao mạch đỏ trên mặt](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/facial-red-vein-and-vascular-birthmark-treatments) bao gồm:
- [Phẫu thuật điện](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/electrosurgery)
- [Ánh sáng xung cường độ cao](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/intense-pulsed-light-therapy) (IPL)
- [Điều trị bằng laser mạch máu](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/lasers-in-dermatology)
- [Xơ cứng](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/sclerotherapy) .
